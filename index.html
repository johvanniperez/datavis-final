Word Whales:
Exploring the Good and the Bad of Word Clouds

Data Viz 

Within the data visualization community, word clouds are not typically seen as the best tool for visualizing data and conveying insights. Some argue that presenting individual words can provide misleading information. Similarly, representing frequent words in larger fonts does not always convey meaning to a person. 

Others, however, see word clouds for their aesthetics and abilitiy to provide context of textual information. Additionally, word clouds can also be used as text mining tool when processing data. Many see potential in word clouds and explore ways to make them more meaningful.

Word Whales Experiment

Multiple word clouds were created with varying attributes such as the size, color, and positioning of text. Additionally, bargraphs were created to compare the word clouds in terms of effectiveness.

Visualizations were surveyed by participants based on which ones they found helpful in understanding context.
